# SDR Outreach Cadence

Design effective multi-channel outreach sequences for sales development.

## Prompt Template

```
Create an SDR outreach cadence for:

**Target:**
- Persona: [Title, responsibilities]
- Company Size: [Employee count/revenue]
- Industry: [Sector]

**Product/Service:** [What you're selling]
**Conversion Goal:** [Meeting/Demo/Call]
**Sequence Length:** [Number of touches over days]
**Channels:** [Email, Phone, LinkedIn, Direct Mail]
**Value Proposition:** [Key message]

Generate:
1. Complete cadence timeline
2. Email templates for each email touch
3. Voicemail scripts
4. LinkedIn touchpoints
5. A/B test variations
```

## Sample 14-Day Cadence

### Day 1
**Email 1 - Value-Led Introduction**
- Subject: Focused on their problem
- Body: Problem statement, brief solution, soft CTA
- Goal: Open rate, establish relevance

### Day 2
**LinkedIn - Connection Request**
- Personalized note referencing email
- No pitch, just connection

### Day 3
**Phone 1 - Introduction Call**
- Follow voicemail script if no answer
- Reference email sent
- Goal: Conversation or callback

### Day 5
**Email 2 - Value Add**
- Share relevant content/insight
- No ask, just value
- Goal: Engagement, click

### Day 7
**Phone 2 - Follow-Up Call**
- Reference previous touches
- New angle/insight
- Goal: Conversation

### Day 8
**LinkedIn - Engage with Content**
- Like/comment on their post
- Or share relevant content
- No direct pitch

### Day 10
**Email 3 - Social Proof**
- Customer story/case study
- Relevant to their industry
- Clear meeting CTA

### Day 12
**Phone 3 - Final Attempt**
- Create urgency
- Different value angle
- Direct ask

### Day 14
**Email 4 - Breakup Email**
- Acknowledge no response
- Leave door open
- Remove from active sequence

## Cadence Best Practices

- Mix channels strategically
- Vary message angles
- Always add value
- Personalize at scale
- Track and iterate
- Respect "no" signals
