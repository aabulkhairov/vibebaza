# Google Ads Copy Generator

Write Google Ads that drive clicks and conversions within strict character limits.

## Prompt Template

```
Create Google Ads for:

**Business/Product:** [What you're promoting]
**Target Keywords:** [Primary keywords]
**Target Audience:** [Who's searching]
**Unique Selling Points:** [3-4 differentiators]
**Offer/CTA:** [What action you want]
**Landing Page:** [Where ad leads]
**Competitors:** [Who you're against]

Generate Responsive Search Ad:
- 15 Headlines (30 chars each)
- 4 Descriptions (90 chars each)
- Display paths (15 chars each)
- Pin recommendations
```

## Responsive Search Ad Structure

### Headlines (30 characters limit)
**Brand Headlines:**
- "[Brand Name] Official Site"
- "Try [Brand] Free Today"

**Benefit Headlines:**
- "Save 10 Hours Per Week"
- "Increase Revenue by 40%"

**Feature Headlines:**
- "AI-Powered Analytics"
- "100+ Integrations"

**CTA Headlines:**
- "Get Your Free Trial"
- "Request a Demo Today"

**Social Proof Headlines:**
- "Trusted by 10,000+ Teams"
- "5-Star Rated Platform"

**Urgency Headlines:**
- "Limited Time: 30% Off"
- "Start Free This Week"

### Descriptions (90 character limit)
**Value-focused:**
"Transform your sales process with AI-powered automation. Start your free trial today."

**Feature-focused:**
"CRM, email automation, and analytics in one platform. No credit card required to start."

**Social proof:**
"Join 50,000+ businesses growing with [Product]. See why teams choose us over competitors."

**Offer-focused:**
"Get 30% off your first year. Enterprise features at startup prices. Cancel anytime."

## Keyword Integration

- Include primary keyword in at least 3 headlines
- Use keyword in first description
- Match keyword intent in messaging

## Testing Strategy

| Test | Variation A | Variation B |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| Hook | Benefit-led | Problem-led |
| CTA | "Free Trial" | "Get Demo" |
| Proof | Stats | Awards |
| Urgency | With | Without |
