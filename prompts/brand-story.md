# Brand Story Generator

Craft a brand story that resonates and differentiates.

## Prompt Template

```
Create a brand story for:

**Company Name:** [Name]
**Industry:** [What you do]
**Founding Story:** [How/why it started]
**Mission:** [What you're trying to accomplish]
**Values:** [Core beliefs]
**Target Audience:** [Who you serve]
**Problem You Solve:** [The change you create]
**Differentiator:** [What makes you unique]

Generate:
1. Brand story (500 words)
2. Condensed version (150 words)
3. About page copy
4. Founder's message
5. Mission statement options
```

## Brand Story Framework

### The Setup
- The world before your solution
- The problem that needed solving
- Why existing solutions fell short

### The Catalyst
- The founding moment/insight
- Who started it and why they cared
- The first step taken

### The Journey
- Challenges overcome
- Pivotal moments
- Growth milestones
- Lessons learned

### The Present
- Where you are now
- Who you've helped
- Impact created

### The Future
- Vision for what's next
- Invitation to join the journey
- Call to action

## Story Elements

### Human Elements
- Founder's personal connection
- Customer transformation stories
- Team passion and expertise

### Emotional Hooks
- Underdog narrative
- David vs. Goliath
- "Aha" moment
- Passion project origin
- Customer-inspired pivot

### Credibility Elements
- Specific numbers/milestones
- Named customers/results
- Recognition/awards
- Expert credentials

## Tone Variations

**Inspirational:** Focus on mission and impact
**Authentic:** Focus on honest journey and lessons
**Professional:** Focus on expertise and results
**Relatable:** Focus on shared struggles and victories
