# Quarterly Business Review (QBR)

Structure impactful QBR presentations that strengthen customer relationships.

## Prompt Template

```
Create QBR content for:

**Customer:**
- Company: [Name]
- Industry: [Sector]
- Account Size: [ARR/Spend]
- Relationship Length: [Time as customer]

**Account Health:**
- Adoption Level: [High/Medium/Low]
- Key Metrics: [Usage, results achieved]
- Challenges: [Any issues]
- Expansion Opportunity: [Upsell potential]

**Attendees:** [Who will be there]
**Meeting Length:** [30/45/60 minutes]

Generate:
1. Agenda
2. Executive summary slide
3. Results/ROI section
4. Product roadmap highlights
5. Recommendations and next steps
6. Discussion questions
```

## QBR Structure

### 1. Welcome & Agenda (5 min)
- Meeting objectives
- Agenda overview
- Introductions if needed

### 2. Executive Summary (5 min)
- Partnership highlights
- Key wins
- Areas of focus

### 3. Results Review (15 min)
- KPIs and metrics
- ROI analysis
- Goal progress
- Comparison to benchmarks

### 4. Product & Support Review (10 min)
- Feature adoption
- Support interactions
- Training completed
- Product roadmap highlights

### 5. Strategic Discussion (15 min)
- Their business priorities
- How we can support
- Recommendations
- Expansion opportunities

### 6. Action Items & Next Steps (10 min)
- Summary of agreements
- Owner assignments
- Next meeting schedule
