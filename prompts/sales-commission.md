# Sales Commission Plan

Create competitive sales compensation structures.

## Prompt Template

```
Design a sales commission plan for:

**Role:** [SDR/AE/AM/Sales Manager]
**Target OTE:** [On-target earnings]
**Base/Variable Split:** [60/40, 50/50, etc.]
**Quota Type:** [Revenue/Meetings/Pipeline]
**Sales Cycle:** [Average deal time]
**ACV Range:** [Deal sizes]

**Business Goals:**
- Primary metric: [What to optimize]
- Secondary metrics: [Other important factors]
- Behaviors to encourage: [Specific actions]

Generate:
1. Compensation structure overview
2. Commission rate tiers
3. Bonus/accelerator structure
4. Clawback provisions
5. Example scenarios at different performance levels
```

## Compensation Components

### Base Salary
- Provides stability
- Typically 50-70% of OTE
- Based on market rate and experience

### Variable Compensation
- Tied to performance
- Motivates achievement
- Typically 30-50% of OTE

### Commission Structures
**Linear:** Same rate for all sales
**Tiered:** Increasing rates at thresholds
**Accelerator:** Higher rates above quota
**Decelerator:** Lower rates below threshold

## Example: Account Executive Plan

**OTE:** $150,000
**Base:** $75,000 (50%)
**Variable:** $75,000 (50%)

**Quota:** $1,000,000 annually

**Commission Structure:**
| Performance | Rate | Payout |
|-------------|------|--------|
| 0-80% | 5% | $0-40K |
| 80-100% | 7.5% | $40K-75K |
| 100-120% | 10% | $75K-95K |
| 120%+ | 12% | $95K+ |

**Accelerators:**
- Multi-year deals: +1% bonus
- New logo: +$500 spiff
- Strategic product: +2% on product revenue

**Example Scenarios:**
- 80% quota ($800K): $75K base + $40K commission = $115K
- 100% quota ($1M): $75K base + $75K commission = $150K
- 120% quota ($1.2M): $75K base + $95K commission = $170K

## SDR Plan Example

**OTE:** $75,000
**Base:** $50,000 (67%)
**Variable:** $25,000 (33%)

**Monthly Quota:** 15 qualified meetings

**Payout:**
- Per meeting: $100
- Tier bonus at 15: $500
- Stretch bonus at 20: $1,000 additional
