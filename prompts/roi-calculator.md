# ROI Calculator Copy

Write copy that makes ROI calculators compelling and credible.

## Prompt Template

```
Create ROI calculator content for:

**Product/Service:** [What you sell]
**Target User:** [Who uses the calculator]
**Key Value Metrics:**
- Metric 1: [e.g., Time saved]
- Metric 2: [e.g., Revenue increase]
- Metric 3: [e.g., Cost reduction]

**Input Variables:** [What users will input]
**Proof Points:** [Data backing your claims]
**Calculation Logic:** [Basic methodology]

Generate:
1. Calculator headline and intro
2. Input field labels and helper text
3. Results section copy
4. Assumptions/methodology disclosure
5. CTA after results
6. Social proof to include
```

## Calculator Structure

### Introduction Section
**Headline:** "See How Much [Product] Could Save You"
**Subhead:** "Answer a few questions to calculate your potential ROI"

### Input Section Copy
**Label Best Practices:**
- Clear, specific questions
- Helper text with examples
- Default values that feel realistic

Examples:
- "How many [users/employees]?" → *Helper: "Full-time employees who [relevant action]"*
- "What's your average [metric]?" → *Helper: "Industry average is [X]"*
- "Hours spent on [task] per week?" → *Helper: "Include all team members"*

### Results Section

**Headline:** "Your Potential Results"

**Metric Display:**
"With [Product], you could see:
- **$[X] saved** in [category] annually
- **[Y] hours** returned to your team each month
- **[Z]% improvement** in [metric]"

**ROI Summary:**
"For an investment of $[price], your potential return is $[savings] — a **[X]x ROI**."

### Methodology Disclosure

"*How we calculate this:*
Based on [data source] and results from [X] customers. Assumes [key assumption]. Individual results may vary based on [factors]."

### Post-Results CTA

"Ready to realize these savings?"
[Get Custom ROI Analysis] [Talk to Sales]

"Want to validate these numbers? Our team can build a custom business case for [Company]."

## Best Practices

- Use conservative estimates
- Show your math
- Include comparison scenarios
- Reference real customer data
- Provide export/share option
