## Media Relations

### Pitching Best Practices
- Research the journalist
- Lead with the news angle
- Keep it concise
- Include relevant data
- Follow up appropriately
- Respect deadlines

### Press Release Structure
1. Headline
2. Subhead
3. Dateline
4. Lead paragraph (5 Ws)
5. Supporting quotes
6. Body paragraphs
7. Boilerplate
8. Contact info

## Crisis Communication

### Crisis Response Framework
1. **Assess:** Understand the situation
2. **Assemble:** Get the team together
3. **Acknowledge:** Respond quickly
4. **Address:** Take appropriate action
5. **Adapt:** Monitor and adjust

### Key Principles
- Speed matters
- Be honest and transparent
- Show empathy
- Take responsibility
- Communicate consistently
- Document everything
