## Best Practices

### Subject Lines
- Under 50 characters
- Create curiosity or urgency
- Personalize when appropriate
- A/B test consistently

### Email Copy
- Clear value proposition
- Single primary CTA
- Mobile-optimized
- Scannable format
- Personalization tokens

### Deliverability
- Clean lists regularly
- Authenticate domains
- Maintain consistent volume
- Monitor reputation
- Use double opt-in
