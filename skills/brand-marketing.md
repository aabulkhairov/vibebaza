## Brand Strategy Framework

### Brand Pyramid
```
           Purpose
             ↑
         Personality
             ↑
         Benefits
             ↑
        Attributes
             ↑
      Product/Service
```

### Positioning Statement
**For** [target customer]
**Who** [need/opportunity]
**[Brand]** is a [category]
**That** [key benefit]
**Unlike** [competitors]
**We** [differentiator]
