# Sales Manager Expert

Leadership expertise in building and managing high-performing sales teams.

## Core Competencies

### Team Leadership
- Hiring and recruiting
- Onboarding and training
- Performance management
- Career development
- Culture building

### Coaching
- 1:1 structure
- Call coaching
- Deal coaching
- Skill development
- Feedback delivery

### Operations
- Forecasting
- Pipeline management
- Territory design
- Quota setting
- Process optimization

## Sales Coaching Framework

### GROW Model
- **Goal:** What do you want to achieve?
- **Reality:** Where are you now?
- **Options:** What could you do?
- **Will:** What will you do?

### Coaching Cadence
| Meeting | Frequency | Focus |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| 1:1s | Weekly | Development, blockers |
| Pipeline Review | Weekly | Deal strategy |
| Call Review | Weekly | Skill building |
| Team Meeting | Weekly | Updates, training |
| Forecast Review | Weekly | Accuracy, commit |

## Performance Management

### Metrics to Track
- Activity metrics (calls, emails)
- Pipeline metrics (created, moved)
- Outcome metrics (won, lost)
- Efficiency metrics (win rate, cycle)
- Behavior metrics (process adherence)

### Performance Review Framework
1. Results vs. quota
2. Activity vs. expectations
3. Skill development progress
4. Behavior and culture fit
5. Career trajectory

## Hiring Framework

### Key Competencies
- Coachability
- Intelligence
- Work ethic
- Prior success
- Culture fit

### Interview Process
1. Phone screen (30 min)
2. Hiring manager (45 min)
3. Role play (30 min)
4. Peer interviews (2x30 min)
5. Executive (30 min)
6. References

## Forecasting Best Practices

- Inspect deals, not spreadsheets
- Focus on commit, not upside
- Validate with customer evidence
- Track historical accuracy
- Build in appropriate cushion
