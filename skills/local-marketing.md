## Google Business Profile Optimization

### Essential Elements
- Complete business information
- Accurate categories
- High-quality photos
- Regular posts
- Q&A management
- Review responses

### Ranking Factors
1. **Relevance:** Match to search intent
2. **Distance:** Proximity to searcher
3. **Prominence:** Online reputation

### Optimization Checklist
- [ ] Verify listing
- [ ] Complete all fields
- [ ] Add 10+ photos
- [ ] Select primary + secondary categories
- [ ] Write keyword-rich description
- [ ] Add products/services
- [ ] Enable messaging
- [ ] Post weekly updates

## Local SEO Strategy

### Citation Building
- NAP consistency (Name, Address, Phone)
- Key directories (Yelp, Yellow Pages)
- Industry-specific directories
- Local business associations
- Chamber of commerce

### Review Strategy
- Ask satisfied customers
- Make it easy (QR codes, links)
- Respond to all reviews
- Address negative reviews professionally
- Never buy reviews
