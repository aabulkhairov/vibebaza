## Pricing Models

### SaaS Pricing
| Model | Description | Best For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Per User | $ per seat | Collaboration tools |
| Usage-Based | $ per unit | Infrastructure, APIs |
| Tiered | Feature-based plans | Broad market |
| Flat Rate | Single price | Simple products |
| Freemium | Free + paid tiers | PLG companies |

## Pricing Psychology

### Psychological Principles
- **Anchoring:** Show higher price first
- **Decoy Effect:** Add option to guide choice
- **Charm Pricing:** $99 vs $100
- **Price Framing:** Monthly vs daily cost
- **Bundling:** Perceived value increase

### Pricing Page Best Practices
- Highlight recommended plan
- Show annual savings
- Lead with value, not price
- Limit choices (3-4 tiers)
- Clear feature comparison

## Pricing Research

### Methods
- Van Westendorp price sensitivity
- Conjoint analysis
- Competitive benchmarking
- Customer interviews
- A/B testing

### Van Westendorp Questions
1. At what price is it too expensive?
2. At what price is it too cheap?
3. At what price is it getting expensive?
4. At what price is it a bargain?
