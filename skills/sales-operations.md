## Sales Process Framework

### Stage Definitions
| Stage | Entry Criteria | Exit Criteria |
|-------|----------------|---------------|
| Discovery | First meeting | Needs confirmed |
| Qualification | BANT qualified | Champion identified |
| Demo/Evaluation | Demo completed | Technical win |
| Proposal | Proposal sent | Verbal agreement |
| Negotiation | Redlines started | Final terms |
| Closed Won | Signature obtained | - |

### Stage Probabilities
- Discovery: 10%
