## Platform Expertise

### Instagram
- Best for: Lifestyle, visual products
- Formats: Posts, Stories, Reels
- Metrics: Reach, saves, shares

### TikTok
- Best for: Gen Z, trends, virality
- Formats: Videos, duets, stitches
- Metrics: Views, completion, shares

### YouTube
- Best for: In-depth content, tutorials
- Formats: Integrations, dedicated, shorts
- Metrics: Views, watch time, clicks

### LinkedIn
- Best for: B2B, thought leadership
- Formats: Posts, articles, videos
- Metrics: Engagement, shares, leads

## Campaign Types

### Product Seeding
- Send product for organic review
- Low cost, lower control
- Good for building relationships

### Sponsored Content
- Paid posts with disclosure
- More control over messaging
- Clear deliverables and timing

### Brand Ambassador
- Long-term partnership
- Multiple content pieces
- Deeper integration
- Exclusivity considerations

### Affiliate/Performance
- Commission-based
- Trackable results
- Lower risk for brand
- Performance incentives
