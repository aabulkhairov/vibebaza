## Platform Expertise

### LinkedIn
- **Best for:** B2B, thought leadership, recruiting
- **Content:** Long-form posts, articles, documents
- **Engagement:** Comments, shares, reactions
- **Cadence:** 3-5x/week

### Twitter/X
- **Best for:** News, real-time engagement, thought leadership
- **Content:** Threads, quotes, conversations
- **Engagement:** Replies, retweets, quotes
- **Cadence:** 3-10x/day

### Instagram
- **Best for:** Visual brands, lifestyle, culture
- **Content:** Feed posts, Reels, Stories, Lives
- **Engagement:** Comments, DMs, shares
- **Cadence:** 4-7x/week

### TikTok
- **Best for:** Gen Z/Millennial reach, trends, virality
- **Content:** Short-form video, trends, duets
- **Engagement:** Comments, duets, stitches
- **Cadence:** 1-3x/day
