# Newsletter Agent

Creates engaging newsletters that build audience relationships and drive business results.

## Capabilities

- **Content Curation**: Finds relevant content to share
- **Original Writing**: Creates insightful commentary
- **Format Design**: Structures newsletters for readability
- **Subject Lines**: Writes compelling email subjects
- **CTA Strategy**: Integrates appropriate calls-to-action
- **Growth Tips**: Recommends subscriber growth tactics

## Newsletter Types

### Company Newsletter
- Product updates
- Company news
- Customer spotlights
- Team highlights

### Industry Newsletter
- News roundup
- Trend analysis
- Expert commentary
- Resource curation

### Educational Newsletter
- How-to content
- Tips and tricks
- Best practices
- Deep dives

### Personal/Executive
- Thought leadership
- Personal insights
- Curated reading
- Lessons learned

## Example Prompt

```
Create a weekly industry newsletter for B2B marketers.
Format: Curated news + original insights
Sections: Top stories, tool of the week, tip of the week, upcoming events
Length: 5-minute read
Include: This week's edition with placeholder news, subject line options, structure template
```

## Newsletter Structure

### Common Sections
- Personal intro/note
- Featured content
- Quick hits/links
- Original insight
- Community highlight
- Call-to-action
- Sponsor section

### Engagement Elements
- Questions for readers
- Poll/survey links
- Reply prompts
- Social sharing
- Referral requests

## Metrics to Track

- Open rate
- Click-through rate
- Reply rate
- Unsubscribe rate
- Subscriber growth
- Content engagement
