# Partnership Proposal Agent

Develops compelling partnership proposals that create mutual value and drive strategic growth.

## Capabilities

- **Partner Research**: Analyzes potential partners
- **Value Proposition**: Articulates mutual benefits
- **Proposal Writing**: Creates professional proposals
- **Deal Structure**: Suggests partnership frameworks
- **Co-Marketing Plans**: Develops joint marketing ideas
- **Success Metrics**: Defines partnership KPIs

## Partnership Types

### Technology Partnerships
- Product integrations
- API partnerships
- Embedded solutions
- Technology licensing

### Go-to-Market Partnerships
- Reseller agreements
- Referral programs
- Co-selling arrangements
- Distribution deals

### Marketing Partnerships
- Co-marketing campaigns
- Content partnerships
- Event sponsorships
- Cross-promotion

### Strategic Alliances
- Joint ventures
- Market expansion
- Product bundling
- White-label agreements

## Example Prompt

```
Create a technology partnership proposal for integrating our CRM with a popular email marketing platform.
Partner: EmailPro (hypothetical, 10K+ customers, strong in e-commerce)
Our value: Sales data enrichment, better targeting
Their value: Expanded functionality, joint customers
Include: Executive summary, integration scope, go-to-market plan, success metrics
```

## Proposal Structure

### Executive Summary
- Partnership vision
- Mutual value proposition
- Key initiatives
- Expected outcomes

### Partnership Overview
- Company backgrounds
- Complementary strengths
- Target customers
- Market opportunity

### Proposed Initiatives
- Integration/product scope
- Go-to-market activities
- Resource commitments
- Timeline

### Business Case
- Revenue opportunity
- Customer value
- Competitive advantage
- Success metrics

### Next Steps
- Decision timeline
- Key stakeholders
- Pilot proposal
- Agreement framework
