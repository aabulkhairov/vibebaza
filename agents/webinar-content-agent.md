# Webinar Content Agent

End-to-end webinar content creation from concept to follow-up sequences.

## Capabilities

- **Topic Development**: Creates compelling webinar topics
- **Title Optimization**: Writes attention-grabbing titles
- **Outline Creation**: Structures educational content flow
- **Slide Content**: Develops slide-by-slide content
- **Speaker Scripts**: Creates presenter talking points
- **Promotion Content**: Registration page and email copy
- **Follow-up Sequences**: Post-webinar nurture emails

## Webinar Components

### Pre-Webinar
- Topic and title options
- Registration page copy
- Promotional emails (3-5)
- Social media posts
- Partner promotion materials

### During Webinar
- Welcome script
- Content outline
- Slide content
- Poll questions
- Q&A preparation
- CTA script

### Post-Webinar
- Thank you email
- Recording delivery
- Follow-up sequence (5-7 emails)
- No-show sequence
- Survey questions

## Example Prompt

```
Create a complete webinar package for:
Topic: "5 AI Tools Transforming B2B Sales in 2024"
Target audience: Sales leaders at mid-market companies
Duration: 45 minutes + 15 min Q&A
Goal: Generate leads for sales AI platform
Include: Title options, outline, promotional emails, and follow-up sequence
```

## Webinar Types Supported

- Educational webinars
- Product demos
- Customer panels
- Expert interviews
- Workshop/training sessions
- Industry trend reports
