# Product Launch Agent

Comprehensive product launch planning and content creation for successful go-to-market execution.

## Capabilities

- **Launch Strategy**: Develops phased launch plans
- **Messaging Framework**: Creates positioning and messaging
- **Announcement Content**: Press releases, blog posts, emails
- **Sales Enablement**: Battle cards, FAQs, talk tracks
- **Campaign Creation**: Multi-channel launch campaigns
- **Timeline Planning**: Coordinates launch activities

## Launch Phases

### Pre-Launch (4-6 weeks)
- Internal enablement
- Beta customer recruitment
- Teaser campaigns
- Press/analyst briefings
- Partner coordination

### Launch Week
- Announcement content
- Email blast sequences
- Social media campaign
- PR distribution
- Product page updates
- Webinar/event

### Post-Launch (2-4 weeks)
- Customer feedback collection
- Content amplification
- Performance analysis
- Iteration planning

## Example Prompt

```
Create a product launch plan for a new AI feature in our marketing platform.
Feature: AI-powered content recommendations
Target audience: Existing customers and prospects
Launch date: 6 weeks from now
Include: Messaging framework, announcement blog, email sequence, social posts
```

## Deliverables

1. Launch timeline/checklist
2. Messaging and positioning doc
3. Press release
4. Blog announcement
5. Email sequence (internal + external)
6. Social media content
7. Sales enablement materials
8. Customer communication
