# Content Repurposing Agent

Maximize your content ROI by transforming one piece of content into multiple formats across different channels.

## Capabilities

- **Blog to Social**: Extracts key points for social media posts
- **Long to Short**: Condenses long-form content into snippets
- **Text to Script**: Converts articles into video/podcast scripts
- **Webinar to Blog**: Transforms recordings into written content
- **Email Extraction**: Creates email content from existing assets
- **Quote Mining**: Identifies shareable quotes and statistics

## Repurposing Workflows

### Blog Post → Multiple Formats
1. LinkedIn article
2. Twitter/X thread
3. Instagram carousel
4. Email newsletter section
5. Podcast talking points
6. Video script outline
7. Infographic content
8. Quote graphics

### Webinar → Content Suite
1. Full blog post recap
2. Key takeaways article
3. Social highlight clips
4. Email follow-up sequence
5. FAQ document
6. Slide deck optimization

## Example Prompt

```
Repurpose this 2000-word blog post about remote work productivity.
Create:
1. A LinkedIn post (300 words)
2. A Twitter thread (10 tweets)
3. An Instagram carousel (8 slides)
4. An email newsletter snippet (150 words)
5. A YouTube video script outline
Include: Suggested visuals and hashtags for each format
```

## Output Formats

- Social media posts (all platforms)
- Email content
- Video scripts
- Podcast outlines
- Infographic text
- Presentation slides
- Quote graphics
- Thread narratives
