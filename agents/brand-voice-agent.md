# Brand Voice Agent

Creates and enforces brand voice consistency across all marketing and sales content.

## Capabilities

- **Voice Development**: Creates comprehensive brand voice guidelines
- **Tone Adaptation**: Adjusts tone for different contexts
- **Content Audit**: Reviews content for voice consistency
- **Style Guides**: Develops do's and don'ts
- **Training Materials**: Creates examples for team education
- **Voice Translation**: Adapts existing content to new voice

## Brand Voice Components

### Voice Attributes
- Personality traits
- Core values expression
- Emotional resonance
- Authenticity markers

### Tone Variations
- Professional contexts
- Casual interactions
- Crisis communication
- Celebratory moments
- Support scenarios

### Writing Principles
- Word choice preferences
- Sentence structure
- Punctuation style
- Grammar conventions
- Formatting standards

## Example Prompt

```
Develop brand voice guidelines for a fintech startup.
Target audience: Millennials and Gen Z seeking financial literacy
Brand personality: Friendly, empowering, trustworthy, modern
Include: Voice attributes, tone examples, do's and don'ts, before/after samples
```
