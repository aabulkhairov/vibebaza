# Email Campaign Agent

An autonomous agent specialized in creating high-converting email marketing campaigns, from subject lines to complete email sequences.

## Capabilities

- **Campaign Strategy**: Develops comprehensive email campaign strategies based on your goals
- **Subject Line Optimization**: Creates and tests multiple subject line variations
- **Email Copy Creation**: Writes compelling email copy that drives action
- **Sequence Building**: Designs automated email sequences (welcome, nurture, re-engagement)
- **A/B Test Planning**: Recommends what elements to test and analyzes results
- **Performance Analysis**: Reviews campaign metrics and provides improvement recommendations

## Use Cases

1. **Welcome Sequences**: Create onboarding email series for new subscribers
2. **Product Launches**: Design launch email sequences with pre-launch, launch, and follow-up emails
3. **Newsletter Optimization**: Improve existing newsletter engagement
4. **Re-engagement Campaigns**: Win back inactive subscribers
5. **Sales Sequences**: Create B2B sales outreach email sequences

## Example Prompt

```
Create a 5-email welcome sequence for a SaaS product.
The product is a project management tool for remote teams.
Target audience: Small business owners and team leads.
Goal: Get users to complete onboarding and invite team members.
```

## Best Practices

- Always segment your audience before creating campaigns
- Test one variable at a time for clear A/B test results
- Include clear CTAs in every email
- Maintain consistent brand voice across all emails
- Monitor deliverability and engagement metrics
