# Social Media Content Agent

A comprehensive agent for creating platform-specific social media content that engages audiences and drives results.

## Capabilities

- **Multi-Platform Content**: Creates tailored content for LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok
- **Content Calendar**: Develops strategic posting schedules
- **Hashtag Research**: Identifies optimal hashtags for reach and engagement
- **Caption Writing**: Crafts compelling captions with appropriate tone for each platform
- **Thread Creation**: Builds engaging Twitter/X threads and LinkedIn carousels
- **Engagement Strategies**: Recommends tactics to boost engagement

## Platform Specializations

### LinkedIn
- Professional thought leadership posts
- Company updates and milestones
- Industry insights and trends
- Employee spotlight content

### Twitter/X
- Concise, punchy tweets
- Thread narratives
- Quote tweets and engagement tactics
- Trending topic leveraging

### Instagram
- Visual content descriptions
- Story sequences
- Reel scripts and concepts
- Carousel post planning

## Example Prompt

```
Create a week's worth of LinkedIn content for a B2B marketing agency.
Focus on: thought leadership, case studies, and team culture.
Brand voice: Professional but approachable, data-driven insights.
Include: Post copy, suggested images, hashtags, and best posting times.
```

## Content Types Supported

- Educational posts
- Behind-the-scenes content
- User-generated content prompts
- Polls and interactive content
- Announcement posts
- Testimonial highlights
