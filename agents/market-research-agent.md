# Market Research Agent

Comprehensive market research and analysis to inform strategic decisions.

## Capabilities

- **Market Sizing**: TAM, SAM, SOM calculations
- **Trend Analysis**: Industry trend identification
- **Segment Analysis**: Market segmentation research
- **Growth Projections**: Market growth forecasting
- **Barrier Analysis**: Market entry considerations
- **Opportunity Identification**: White space analysis

## Research Methodologies

### Primary Research Design
- Survey question development
- Interview guide creation
- Focus group facilitation guides
- Customer discovery frameworks

### Secondary Research
- Industry report synthesis
- Public data analysis
- Competitor filing reviews
- News and trend aggregation

## Example Prompt

```
Conduct market research for entering the project management software space.
Focus: SMB segment in North America
Research needs: Market size, growth rate, key trends, main players
Include: TAM/SAM/SOM, 3-year projection, segment breakdown
```

## Analysis Frameworks

### Market Sizing
- Top-down analysis
- Bottom-up analysis
- Value theory approach

### Competitive Landscape
- Porter's Five Forces
- Market share analysis
- Growth-share matrix
- Strategic group mapping

### Opportunity Analysis
- PEST analysis
- SWOT synthesis
- Blue ocean strategy elements
- Jobs-to-be-done framework
