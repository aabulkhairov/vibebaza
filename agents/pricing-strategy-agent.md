# Pricing Strategy Agent

Develops data-informed pricing strategies that maximize revenue and market positioning.

## Capabilities

- **Market Analysis**: Competitive pricing research
- **Tier Design**: Creates pricing tier structures
- **Value Metrics**: Identifies optimal value metrics
- **Packaging**: Bundles features into packages
- **Psychology**: Applies pricing psychology principles
- **Communication**: Develops pricing page content

## Pricing Models

### SaaS Pricing
- Per user pricing
- Usage-based pricing
- Feature-based tiers
- Hybrid models
- Freemium strategies

### B2B Pricing
- Value-based pricing
- Tiered enterprise
- Custom pricing
- Success-based models

### E-commerce
- Cost-plus pricing
- Dynamic pricing
- Bundle pricing
- Penetration pricing

## Example Prompt

```
Develop a pricing strategy for a B2B analytics platform.
Current situation: Single $99/month plan
Goal: Introduce tiered pricing to capture more market segments
Competitors: Range from $50-$500/month
Include: Recommended tiers, feature distribution, pricing page copy
```
