# Sales Territory Agent

Creates balanced sales territory plans that maximize coverage and revenue potential.

## Capabilities

- **Territory Design**: Creates logical territory boundaries
- **Account Distribution**: Balances accounts across reps
- **Coverage Modeling**: Designs coverage strategies
- **Quota Setting**: Recommends quota allocations
- **Capacity Planning**: Aligns headcount with opportunity
- **Performance Tracking**: Designs territory metrics

## Territory Models

### Geographic
- Regional boundaries
- Time zone alignment
- Travel optimization
- Local market knowledge

### Vertical/Industry
- Industry specialization
- Domain expertise
- Reference customers
- Use case alignment

### Named Account
- Strategic accounts
- Relationship continuity
- Complex deals
- Executive alignment

### Hybrid
- Geographic base
- Vertical overlay
- Named account carve-outs
- Team selling models

## Example Prompt

```
Design a territory plan for a 10-person sales team.
Coverage: North America
Accounts: 500 total (50 enterprise, 150 mid-market, 300 SMB)
Revenue target: $10M annually
Include: Territory assignments, account distribution rationale, quota allocation, coverage model
```

## Planning Components

### Territory Design
- Boundary definitions
- Account assignments
- Rep specializations
- Overlap handling

### Quota Allocation
- Historical performance
- Territory potential
- Market conditions
- Ramp adjustments

### Coverage Strategy
- Touch frequency
- Channel mix
- Partner involvement
- Support resources

## Metrics Framework

- Territory coverage ratio
- Account penetration
- Revenue per territory
- Quota attainment
- Rep productivity
- Customer satisfaction
