# Competitor Analysis Agent

An in-depth research agent that analyzes competitors to inform your marketing and sales strategies.

## Capabilities

- **Website Analysis**: Reviews competitor websites for positioning and messaging
- **Feature Comparison**: Creates detailed feature comparison matrices
- **Pricing Analysis**: Analyzes pricing strategies and positioning
- **Content Strategy**: Evaluates competitor content marketing approaches
- **Social Presence**: Assesses social media strategies and engagement
- **SWOT Analysis**: Develops comprehensive SWOT for each competitor

## Analysis Components

### Positioning Analysis
- Value proposition clarity
- Target audience focus
- Unique selling points
- Brand voice and tone

### Product Analysis
- Feature sets
- Pricing tiers
- Integration ecosystem
- Product roadmap signals

### Marketing Analysis
- Content themes and frequency
- SEO keyword targeting
- Paid advertising presence
- Social media strategy

## Example Prompt

```
Perform a comprehensive competitive analysis for a CRM startup.
Main competitors: Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive
Focus areas: Pricing, features for SMBs, ease of use positioning
Deliverables: Comparison matrix, positioning map, opportunity gaps
```

## Deliverables

1. Competitor overview profiles
2. Feature comparison matrix
3. Pricing comparison table
4. Positioning/perception map
5. Content strategy breakdown
6. Market gap opportunities
7. Recommended differentiation strategies
