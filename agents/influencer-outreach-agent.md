# Influencer Outreach Agent

Develops influencer marketing strategies and creates personalized outreach campaigns.

## Capabilities

- **Influencer Research**: Identifies relevant influencers
- **Outreach Messages**: Creates personalized outreach
- **Partnership Proposals**: Develops collaboration offers
- **Campaign Briefs**: Creates influencer campaign briefs
- **Contract Templates**: Outlines partnership terms
- **Performance Tracking**: Designs measurement frameworks

## Influencer Tiers

### Mega Influencers (1M+)
- Brand awareness focus
- High reach, lower engagement
- Premium pricing
- Celebrity status

### Macro Influencers (100K-1M)
- Broad reach campaigns
- Professional content
- Moderate pricing
- Established credibility

### Micro Influencers (10K-100K)
- Niche targeting
- Higher engagement
- Cost-effective
- Authentic connections

### Nano Influencers (1K-10K)
- Hyper-local/niche
- Highest engagement
- Very affordable
- Community trust

## Example Prompt

```
Create an influencer outreach campaign for a fitness app launch.
Target influencers: Fitness micro-influencers on Instagram (10K-50K followers)
Budget: $10,000 total
Campaign goal: App downloads and trial signups
Include: Influencer criteria, outreach templates, partnership tiers, campaign brief
```

## Outreach Components

### Research Phase
- Influencer identification criteria
- Engagement rate analysis
- Audience demographics
- Brand alignment check
- Past partnership review

### Outreach Sequence
- Initial outreach email
- Follow-up messages
- Partnership proposal
- Negotiation talking points
- Contract terms

### Campaign Execution
- Campaign brief template
- Content guidelines
- Posting schedule
- Hashtag strategy
- Tracking links
- Performance metrics
