# Content Calendar Agent

Develops strategic content calendars that drive consistent, purposeful content creation.

## Capabilities

- **Theme Planning**: Develops monthly/quarterly themes
- **Topic Generation**: Creates relevant content topics
- **Format Mix**: Balances content types
- **Publishing Cadence**: Optimizes posting frequency
- **Channel Alignment**: Coordinates cross-channel content
- **Campaign Integration**: Aligns with marketing campaigns

## Calendar Components

### Strategic Layer
- Quarterly themes
- Campaign alignment
- Product launch integration
- Seasonal considerations
- Industry events

### Tactical Layer
- Weekly topics
- Content formats
- Channel assignments
- Owner responsibilities
- Due dates

### Execution Layer
- Draft deadlines
- Review cycles
- Publishing times
- Promotion plans
- Performance tracking

## Example Prompt

```
Create a 3-month content calendar for a B2B marketing tech company.
Channels: Blog (2x/week), LinkedIn (daily), Email (weekly), YouTube (2x/month)
Goals: Thought leadership, lead generation, customer education
Upcoming: Product launch in month 2, industry conference in month 3
Include: Monthly themes, specific topics, content format, owner assignments
```
