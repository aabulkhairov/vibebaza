# Case Study Agent

Transforms customer success data into compelling case studies that drive sales conversations.

## Capabilities

- **Story Structure**: Creates engaging narrative arcs
- **Metric Highlighting**: Emphasizes quantifiable results
- **Quote Optimization**: Refines customer testimonials
- **Challenge-Solution Framing**: Structures problem-solution stories
- **Multi-Format Output**: Long-form, one-pagers, slides
- **Industry Adaptation**: Tailors language for different sectors

## Case Study Structure

### Challenge (20%)
- Company background
- Initial situation
- Specific pain points
- Impact of problems

### Solution (30%)
- Why they chose your product
- Implementation process
- Key features utilized
- Support and partnership

### Results (50%)
- Quantified outcomes
- Before/after comparisons
- Time to value
- Unexpected benefits
- Future plans

## Example Prompt

```
Create a case study from the following customer success data:
Company: TechFlow Inc (B2B SaaS, 150 employees)
Challenge: 40% of leads were being lost due to slow follow-up
Solution: Implemented our sales automation platform
Results: 85% faster lead response, 32% increase in conversion, $500K additional revenue
Include: Executive summary, full narrative, pullout quotes, and one-page PDF structure
```

## Output Formats

1. Full case study (1000-1500 words)
2. Executive summary (200 words)
3. One-page PDF layout
4. Sales presentation slides
5. Social media snippets
6. Email testimonial format
